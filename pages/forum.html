Forum Guidelines

Be Respectful

Treat others with respect and empathy. We're all here to support each other through difficult times.

Protect Privacy

Do not share personal identifying information of yourself or others. Maintain anonymity when necessary.

Stay Factual

Share accurate information and experiences. Avoid spreading misinformation or unverified claims.

Be Supportive

Offer constructive advice and emotional support. Remember that everyone's situation is unique.
